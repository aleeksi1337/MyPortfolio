Learning

In Unit 1, "Introduction to Computing," we start with the basics, learning about computers, how they work, and the steps and rules (or algorithms) they follow. We also discuss the difference between data and information, with extra materials to help you understand these concepts. In Unit 2, "Number Systems," we look at different ways to represent numbers. We begin with Decimal and Binary systems, then move on to One's and Two's Complement, followed by Octal and Hexadecimal systems, and finish with methods for converting between these systems (Radix Conversion).
